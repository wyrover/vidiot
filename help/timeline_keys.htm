Keyboard actions in the timeline

  • Delete - Delete clips and replace by emptyness
    • Drag & Shift - Replace drag point (useful to make snapping better: the snap point closest to the mouse is considered first. Useful to move over between audio/video track movement also.)
  • Drag & Ctrl - Disable snapping
  • Drag & M (once) - Move clips to make room for dragged clips
  • Drag & M (twice) - Undo make room
